I'm a Doctor of Science researcher at **Tampere University's Gamification Group** in Finland, working on **XR, haptics and the metaverse**. I grew up in Greater Noida with massive stage fright and a Pentium 3, sucked at everything until one moment in my third year of college where *something snapped* — and I've been on a winning streak ever since. Two patents, a published paper, a demo at **CHI 2026**, and a trophy shelf that keeps needing a bigger shelf. I chose a ₹3 LPA job at SAP over an ₹8 LPA one at Infosys, because the *joy of creating* beat the paycheck. Naruto raised me. Tony Stark is the goal. Ronaldo taught me the mentality.

## 🎬 the arc

> **Origin** — a underconfident kid in Greater Noida who won school contests only because braver friends dragged him on stage.
> **The snap** — one blacked-out presentation in year 3, and *"suddenly Shivang Gupta was ready for the spotlight."*
> **Now** — globe-trotting D.Sc who wins hackathons wherever he lands, building a startup to change the world.
